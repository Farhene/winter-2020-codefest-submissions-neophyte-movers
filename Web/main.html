In this educational game platform, you will start by learning the terms. The first button, after you click start, is to learn the climate and energy-type terms. The second button is there to test you on your climate terms. Lastly, the third button is to test you on the energy-type terms. Each test will consist of several questions related to the terms. You will have 30 seconds to answer each question. Good Luck!
